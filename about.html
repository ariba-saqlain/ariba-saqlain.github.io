About
Many people ask, why computer science?
Well, growing up I've always been interested in computer games, different types of technology, and how they work. I decided to take a programming course in high school and not even one week in, I had made up my mind- I wanted to do this for the rest of my life! I was amazed by the concept of writing a few lines of code and seeing a blank screen transform into a dynamic, compelling program.
