About
Many people ask, why Computer Science?

Well growing up I've always been interested in computer games, different types of technology, and how they work. I decided to take a programming course in high school and not even one week in, I had already made up my mind- I wanted to do this for the rest of my life! I was amazed by the concept of writing a few lines of code and seeing a blank screen transform into a dynamic, compelling program.

Currently, I'm studying Computer Science at Wilfrid Laurier University. I have also worked at Manulife Financial as a Test Developer Co-op in the Quality Assurance Department. Through this position, I gained an excellent understanding of software and testing life cycles. Moving forward, I would love the opportunity to work at an organization where I can showcase my wide variety of skills.
