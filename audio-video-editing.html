    Audio/Video Editing Projects

    Classic Film High Tempo Cut

    For this project, I used footage from the 1978 film "Grease" to create a dynamic music video. This involved accurately timing each scene to merge with the beat and tempo of the music, which I chose to be the song "You Don't Know Me" by Jax Jones ft. RAYE. I also employed a variety of visual effects such as transitions filters, key framing, and color correction.

    Movie Trailer Recut

    The 2002 film "Ice Age" is known to be an adventurous comedy for the whole family to enjoy. For this project, I re-cut the footage of this film into a movie trailer that gives the impression that "Ice Age" belongs to a different genre. I added dark overtones to all the scenes, zoomed in at specific times to add emphasis, and matched each scene to a slow and suspenseful soundtrack. Through these techniques, I was able to turn a comedy about three friends trying to return a baby to his father, into a suspenseful thriller about a mentally ill sloth.
